About Me

I began my TEEF course during the 12th grade at the age of 17, managing both high school and the course simultaneously. Before completing the course, I earned my Level 2 Pilates certification. After finishing my studies, I launched my own company, Iron Taurus. As part of this venture, I started teaching fitness classes at Fitness Hut, where I have worked since 2022. Additionally, I worked as a personal trainer for six months at two different gyms. I later paused personal training to focus on college but continued teaching classes. During the first year of my college degree in Computer Engineering and Electronics, I did a professional course at MasterD for Video Games Programming along with other online courses.
